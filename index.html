H8FUL

A Retrofuturistic top-down shooter in which the player is looking for their head through a variety of different levels, after it forcibly swapped with an animal head, which gave them special powers.

Finished product visible in trailer after 0:30, has yet to be published. (No publisher found)

Worked on main gameplay features such as gamelogic, player, gunplay, destruction, player progression and implementing effects and animations. As the project progressed I also worked with the enemy and boss AI.

Progress trailer: Youtube
Made with Unreal Engine 4.
Teamsize of 8.

Augur of Light

Augur of Light is a 1st person puzzle game, where you adventure on a Finnish island solving puzzles. During the first wave of Covid-19 in Finland, we made this game as a university project about coping mechanisms for depression. We worked together with mental industry professionals.

I was part of the implementation team tasked with implementing prototyped content. I also programmed the player, inventory, UI, menues and the world interaction the player could perform.

The game can be found in: Itch.Io
Made with Unity.
Teamsize of 12.

Midnight Ruins

A 2D tower defence game, where the player is tasked with drawing magical runes to create spells. These spells can be used to slow down and destroy approaching threats. Gold is gained and can be used to improve the tower and spells, and to unlock new spells.

Worked on the gameplay functionality, enemy behaviors and collision, balancing and upgrade system.

The game can be downloaded from Google Playstore
Made with Unreal Engine 4.
Teamsize: 4
